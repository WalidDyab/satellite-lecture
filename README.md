# SatThon Digital Museum

A bilingual educational portal for amateur satellite communication, satellite tracking, and SatThon technical training.

---

## Live Website

🌐 https://waliddyab.github.io/satellite-lecture/

---

## Project Overview

SatThon Digital Museum was developed as an interactive learning platform to support participants, engineering students, amateur radio operators, and space technology enthusiasts.

The portal transforms a traditional lecture into a reusable digital experience featuring bilingual content, visual learning, quizzes, and authentic mission-related media.
